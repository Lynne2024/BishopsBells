Bishops Bells Handbell Ringers

Come and see us in concert on 23 November 2024

Bishops Bells is a team of fifteen ringers who rehearse weekly, on a Tuesday evening, at St Peter's Church in Bishops Waltham. We are a very friendly group and always keen to welcome new ringers.

We regularly perform at the Womens' Institute, social groups for the elderly and the disabled, childrens' groups and church groups. These performances include a talk on the history of Bishops Bells and handbell ringing, a demonstration of handbell ringing by six or more members of the team, and the opportunity for the audience to have a go.

The whole team performs at the Christmas services at St Peter's Church, and occasionally takes part in other concerts

This year, 2024, is a very special year for Bishops Bells as we are celebrating our 40th birthday. We held a rally in June, where we invited local handbell teams to come and spend the day playing with us - and eating cake! On 23 November, we are holding a 40th birthday concert with Flautissimo: The Southampton Flute Orchestras, who are also celebrating their 40th birthday.
